# Business Questions Answered

## 🔹 Generic
- How many unique cities does the data have?
- In which city is each branch located?

## 🔹 Product Analysis
- How many unique product lines?
- What is the most selling product line?
- What is the most common payment method?
- What product line had the largest revenue?
- What is the total revenue by month?
- What month had the largest COGS?
- Which city generated the most revenue?
- Which product line had the largest VAT?
- Which product lines perform above/below average?
- What is the average rating per product line?

## 🔹 Sales Analysis
- How many sales occur in each time-of-day/weekday combination?
- Which customer type generates the most revenue?
- Which city contributes the most VAT?
- Which customer type pays the most VAT?

## 🔹 Customer Analysis
- How many unique customer types exist?
- What is the most common customer type?
- Which customer type buys the most units?
- What is the gender distribution of customers?
- How does gender distribution vary by branch?
- When do customers give the most ratings?
- Which day of week has the best average ratings?
